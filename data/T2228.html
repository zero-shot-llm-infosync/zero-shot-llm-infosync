Tetanus and Lockjaw are the same disease.

Lockjaw can be prevented with the tetanus vaccine.

Muscle spasms, fever, and headache are risk factors of tetanus.

Tetanus is an infectious disease that can show up anywhere from a few days to almost a month.

Most patients experience Tetanus for 15 days.

Most of the time tetanus is highly fatal.

In 2015, less than 1/2 the people who contracted lockjaw died from the disease.

Lockjaw has been eradicated in the United States.

Lockjaw is an uncurable terminal disease.
