Geto Onsen is a traditional ryokan with "onsen" - natural hot spring baths.

It was founded in 1134 and is located in Kitakami, Iwate Prefecture, Japan.

The area is in the center of Kurikoma Quasi-National Park and the hotel is located near the narrow and shallow river with a crystal clear water.

Geto Onsen has been around for over 800 years.
