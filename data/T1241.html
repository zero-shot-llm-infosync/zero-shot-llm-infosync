Atari 2600 is an American video game console.

Atari 2600 came with multiple controller inputs included.

Atari 2600 was sold only domestically.

Atari 2600 also known as Atari VCS is manufactured Atari, Inc. is a second generation home video game console.

Atari 2600's introductory price was US$199 in 1977 and it was the most expensive home video game console at that time.

Atary 2600 had 64 bytes RAM and 16-bit MOS Technology 6507 @ 1.19 MHz.

The Atari 2600 was discontinued 14 years after being released in Europe.

Atari 2600 cost $199 when it was discontinued.

Consoles with 128 bytes of RAM are not compatible with joysticks.
