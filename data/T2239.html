Vesak is known by at least a half dozen official names.

Vesak is observed by more Buddhists than Hindus worldwide.

Vesak is celebrated on the same date each year.

Buddha Day occurs on an annual basis.

Buddha Day is celebrated by all of Asia.

In 2018, Buddha Day was celebrated on the same day for every country in Asia.

Vesak is an annual holiday and was celebrated on May 19 in 2019.

Vesak is a religious holiday celebrating Gautama Buddha.

Vesak is celebrated during the winter months.
