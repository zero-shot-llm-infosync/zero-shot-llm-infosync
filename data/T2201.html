Diagnostic methodBlood tests, X-rays
Differential diagnosisFanconi syndrome, scurvy, Lowe syndrome, osteomalacia
PreventionVitamin D supplements for exclusively-breastfed babies
TreatmentVitamin D and calcium
FrequencyRelatively common (Middle East, Africa, Asia)
