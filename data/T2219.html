Sudan Airways Flight 139 crashed in the summer of 2003.

Most of the passengers aboard Flight 139 were citizens of Sudan.

More than 50% of crew and passengers aboard Flight 139 were eventually rescued within of a few miles of Khartoum International Airport.

The crew and passengers totaled 117

Sudan Airways only used Boeing 737-200C

There were fewer fatalities than passengers

Sudan Airways Flight 139 was flying to Khartoum International Airport on July 8, 2003.

Sudan Airways Flight 139 was a Boeing with the aircraft name White Nile.

There were 117 passengers and 106 fatalities with the crash of Sudan Airways Flight 139.
