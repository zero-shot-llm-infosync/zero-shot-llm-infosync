Mawlid is a public holiday in more than thirty countries.

Mawlid is celebrated on either the 10th or 15th of November every year.

Mawlid is celebrated exclusively by adherents of Sunni and Shia Islam.

Mawlid has different observed dates depending on Sunni or Shia background.

Mawlid is celebrated by several continents.

Mawlid is a Hindu-based event.

Mawlid is an Islamic observance associated with the birth of Muhammad.

Mawlid is the most popular Islamic holiday in the world.

Mawlid is celebrated by Christian countries as well.
