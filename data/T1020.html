The chamois coloured goat is a species of goat that resides in Europe.

The goat's use goes beyond milk, as it's fur is also used to make clothing and bags.

The chamois coloured goat is an endangered species and on the brink of extinction.

The Chamois Coloured Goat originated in Switzerland and is used for milking.

The Chamois Coloured Goat was first distributed to Italy in 2000.

The Chamois Coloured Goat is used for milk in England and France.
