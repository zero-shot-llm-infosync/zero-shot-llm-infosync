Jenga is a game of physical skill designed by Leslie Scott.

Jenga can be played between 1 or more players i.e. even 10 players can play Jenga at once.

Play time of Jenga is 20-30 minutes and setup time is more than 5 minutes.

Jenga was designed by Leslie Scott to be used by at least one player.

Jenga is one of the most manual dexterity-intensive games.

It takes longer to setup Jenga than to play a game on the low side of average playing time.

Jenga is a board game that takes little time to play but requires many physical skills and attributes to play well.

A woman is the designer of this board game.

It takes quite a bit of time to both set up and play Jenga.
