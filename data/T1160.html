Lysol is a disinfectant and all-purpose cleaner owned by Reckitt Benckiser in the U.S.

Lysol was introduced in 1889 and it was the first cleaner introduced by Lehn & Fink.

The tagline of Lysol is "100% germ protection".

The Lysol company has been making disinfectant cleaners for over 120 years.

Reckitt Benckiser recently purchased the company from Lehn & Fink.

Lysol brand disinfectant cleaners are sold only in the United States.

Reckitt Benckiser makes all-purpose cleaner products

Lehn & Fink made Lysol available worldwide

Lysol is only available in the U.S.
