Chaco Culture National Historical Park is an UNESCO World Heritage Site and is located in San Juan County and McKinley County, New Mexico, US.

Chaco Culture National Historical Park is the only historic district designated by U.S. National Register of Historic Places.

Chaco Culture National Historical Park has total are more than 35,000 acres.

Chaco Culture National Historical Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in America.

Chaco Culture National Historical Park is free to the public to visit.

Chaco Culture National Historical Park covers parts of Arizona and New Mexico.

Chaco Culture National Historical Park was a U.S. National Monument for more than 70 years.

More of Chaco Culture National Historical Park is located in McKinley County than San Juan County.

Chaco Culture National Historical Park was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places after it was put on the N.M. State Register of Cultural Properties.
