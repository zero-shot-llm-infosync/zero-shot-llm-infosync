The ERJ family is a series of twin-engine long-haul Intercontinental airplane. jets produced by Embraer, a Brazilian aerospace company.

ERJ family's primary users are ExpressJet, Envoy Air, Trans States Airlines and Piedmont Airlines only.

ERJ family has three variants and its unit cost is US$15 Million.

The ERJ family first flew in the mid 1990s.

Envoy Air uses more aircraft from the ERJ family than ExpressJet.

More than 1000 aircraft in the ERJ family have ever been built.

ERJ family ERJ135 / ERJ140 / ERJ145 is a Italian airplane.

Originally developed and produced in Brazil, the ERJ family ERJ135 / ERJ140 / ERJ145 is now solely produced in China.

ERJ family ERJ135 / ERJ140 / ERJ145 was a short-haul Regional airplane.
