The Chechen Republic of Ichkeria is a former province of the USSR.

The Chechen Republic of Ichkeria was the first province of the USSR to declare independence right around the dissolution of the USSR.

The Chechen Republic of Ichkeria never had war with the former USSR after it declared independence.

Chechen Republic of Ichkeria changed from secular to religious.

Chechen Republic of Ichkeria became a government-in-exile in May 2000.

Chechen Republic of Ichkeria declared independence after the dissolution of the USSR.

Chechen Republic of Ichkeria is a war-torn region of Russia.

Chechen Republic of Ichkeria is a contested region of Russia between Islamic separatists and Russian forces.

Chechen Republic of Ichkeria is a secular state with a peaceful populace.
