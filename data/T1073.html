The Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic had both Alexander Chervyakov and Stanislav Shushkevich as its Head of State.

The largest Soviet Socialist Republic was the Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic.

The Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic joined the UN before it was admitted to the Soviet Union.
