The Republic of Chad is a country where its people mostly speak Arabic or French.

The Republic of Chad is the 2nd most populous country in Africa.

The Republic of Chad resides south of the equator.

In 2014-2015, 1.7 percent of the population of the Republic of Chad was an unspecified ethnic group.

Arabic is spoken more commonly than French in the Republic of Chad.

Idriss Deby is the President of the National Assembly.

The Republic of Chad has more than 5 ethnic groups and more than one official language.

Idriss Deby was president of the national assembly prior to becoming the The Republic of Chad's president.

The Republic of Chad has less Arabs than Masalits.
