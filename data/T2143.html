The Joint Entrance Examination is offered biannually.

The National Testing Agency only administers the Joint Entrance Examination.

The Joint Entrance Examination started being given in the 20th century.

The JEE Main started in the 2000s.

The JEE Main has increased in number of tests taken over the years.

The JEE Main only has a positive scoring range.

It takes 6 hours to complete all the papers required for the JEE Main.

The JEE Main includes differential calculus.

You can take the exam every time it is offered for 2 years in a row.
