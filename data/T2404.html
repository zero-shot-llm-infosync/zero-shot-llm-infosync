The handball tournaments at the 2012 Olympic Games in London.

It was held from 28 July to 12 August in the Olympic Park.

The gold medals were won by France (men) and Norway (women).

Handball took place in the summer part of the XXX Olympiad.

The handball final was a very tight match.

More than 50 teams competed at 3 venues in the handball competition.

Five different confederations competed at the Handball games at the Games of the XXX Olympiad.

The majority of the teams were from 2 confederations.

Only 1 venue was used at the Handball games at the Games of the XXX Olympiad.
