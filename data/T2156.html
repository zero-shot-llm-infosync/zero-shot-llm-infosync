The birth anniversary of Lord Mahavir is celebrated in the spring.

The birth anniversary of Mahaveer Swami won't be celebrated in 2019.

The birth anniversary of Mahaveer Swami requires people to bring gifts.

Mahaveer Swami is important in the Jains religion.

Prayers are made only in the Jain Temple.

There are no Jain Temples in India.

Mahaveer Janma Kalyanak is a holiday that happens annually in India.

Mahaveer Janma Kalyanak always happens on the same day each year.

In 2019 Mahaveer Janma Kalyanak happened in May.
