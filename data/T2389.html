At the 1996 Summer Olympics, two different gymnastics disciplines were contested.

The artistic gymnastics events were held at the Georgia Dome from July 20–25 and July 28–29.

The women’s rhythmic group all-around was contested for the first time at these Games.

The 26th Olympic gymnastics events were held at two different venues.

The artistic gymnastics drew more viewers than the rythmic gymnastics did.

The events lasted less than two weeks.

The rhythmic portion of the gymnastic events were held at the Stegeman Coliseum.

The rhythmic portion of the gymnastic events were held on July 20, 1996.

The Gymnastic games of the XXVI Olympiad were held in October, 1996.
