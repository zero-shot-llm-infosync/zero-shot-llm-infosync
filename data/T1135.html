Himeji Castle is a Japanese castle its type is Azuchi-Momoyama castle.

Wood, stone, plaster and tile are the only materials used in the construction and renovation of Himeji Castle.

Restoration work for preservation of Himeji Castle is still going on.

An Azuchi-Momoyama castle can be over 150 feet tall.

Plaster was added to Himeji Castle first by Toyotomi Hideyoshi.

Himeji Castle became a UNESCO World Heritage Site while it was still in use.

Himeji Castle is located in Asia.

Himeji Castle was built only for soldiers.

Himeji Castle is built from a single stone.
