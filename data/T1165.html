The Non-Violence Project is an educational Non-profit organisation founded in 1993 and its focus is on Education, Non-violence and Peace.

The Non-Violence Project has only one headquarter worldwide situated in Geneva, Switzerland.

nonviolence.org is the official website of the Non-Violence Project.

The Non-Violence Project was founded in the early 1990's.

The Non-Violence Project has more women directors than men.

The Non-Violence Project has headquarters in Canada.

The Non-Violence Project was started late in the 20th century.

If Jan Hellman steps down as Chairman of the Non-Violence Project, Rolf Skjoldebrand will take the position.

The Non-Violence Project's headquarters are in North America.
