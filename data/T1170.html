Camelot is a British-bred race horse foaled on 15 March 2009.

Camelot is breeder of Sheikh Abdulla Bin Isa Al-Khalifa.

Camelot was trained by Derrick Smith, Mrs John Magnier and Michael Tabor.

Derrick Smith and Michael Tabor are the owners of Camelot.

Camelot is a Bay Stallion born in the spring of 2009.

The Sire and Grandsire of Camelot were Tarfah and Kingmambo, respectively.

Camelot is a bay-coloured stallion.

Trained by A P O'Brien, the horse was still alive in 2019.

With Sheikh Abdulla Bin Isa Al-Khalifa breeding him, Camelot was born in July and has a record of 9:6-3-1.
