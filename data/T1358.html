The Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AAAI) is an international scientific society devoted to promote research in the field of Artificial Intelligence.

Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence has worldwide location and has office in every country.

Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence was formed 50 years ago in 1969.

AAI began its work in Artificial intelligence prior to 1980.

AAI has been headquartered in Palo Alto, CA for 40 years..

Thomas G. Dietterich succeeded Subbarao Kambhampati as president.

The Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence has headquarters on the West coast of the U.S.

Yolanda Gil will take over for Subbarao Kambhampati as President for the Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence in the near future.

The Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence will always have a male President.
