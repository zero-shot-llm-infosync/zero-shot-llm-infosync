Ten percent of people experience exploding head syndrome, otherwise known as episodic cranial sensory shock.

Exploding head syndrome has a short duration and the causes are unknown.

PTSD is the only diagnosis for exploding head syndrome.

Exploding head syndrome is a medical condition that affects people when falling asleep or waking up.

Exploding head syndrome sufferers scream when woken up.

Exploding head syndrome is a nearly fatal disease if not treated.

Exploding head syndrome is characterized by unusual auditory experiences.

Exploding head syndrome usually requires treatment.
