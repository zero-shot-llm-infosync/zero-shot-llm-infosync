Vidyasagar Setu is a six-lane toll bridge.

Vidyasagar Setu cost billions of US dollars.

Vidyasagar Setu is known for its unique cantilever design.

Vidyasagar Setu is also known as Second Hooghly Bridge and is a two-way with three lanes.

The Braithwaite Burn and Jessop Construction Company Limited is the only company who was involved in the construction of the bridge.

The Vidyasagar Setu opened in the spring of 1992.

Vidyasagar Setu has six lanes in total.

Vehicles using Vidyasagar Setu pay a toll for travelling in both directions.

Vidyasagar Setu costs nothing for vehicles to use.
