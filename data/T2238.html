Vaisakhi, Baisakhi Punjabi: ਵਿਸਾਖੀ is a religious observance by both Sikhs and Hindus.

Vaisakhi, Baisakhi Punjabi: ਵਿਸਾਖੀ shoot fireworks and exchange gifts as part of the celebrations.

Vaisakhi, Baisakhi Punjabi: ਵਿਸਾਖੀ always fall on the same date every year.

Vaisakhi is known by 4 different names.

Vaisakhi is observed on the 13th or 14th of April every year.

Vaisakhi is strictly a cultural observation.

The Baisakh takes place at around the same time every year.

The Baisakh has the same observances for all who celebrate.

Baisakh occurs on the same day every year.
