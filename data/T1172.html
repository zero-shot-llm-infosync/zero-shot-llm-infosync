The Ilyushin Il-96 is a widebody jet airliner manufactured by Voronezh Aircraft Production Association.

The Ilyushin Il-96 is the only widebody jet airliner manufactured by Voronezh Aircraft Production Association.

Ilyushin Il-96's first flight was on 28 September 1990 and its unit cost is US$ 60Ð70 million

Il-96 was introduced about four years after it was first flown.

Il-96 was an improvement on the Ilyushin Il-86 model.

Il-96 has been flying for less than 30 years.

Il-96 is still in production..

Il-96 is the fastest jet airliner in production.

Il-96 cost over 100 million dollars to make.
