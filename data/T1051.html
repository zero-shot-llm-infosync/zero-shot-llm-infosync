Western boxing involves punching and striking.

Bare-knuckle boxing is a prehistoric form of boxing.

The modern form of boxing started in the late 1900's.

Boxing is a sport focused on punching and striking foes.

Boxing has changed greatly over the years, now requiring clothes and shorts where as boxers fought nude in ancient Greece.

Boxing has its origins as an Olympic sport starting a millennia ago.

Boxing has been a modern day Olympic event for over 100 years.

The Olympic sport of boxing has evolved little since its inclusion in the ancient Olympic games.

Despite its popularity, boxing has only been officially recognized as an Olympic sport for 62 years.
