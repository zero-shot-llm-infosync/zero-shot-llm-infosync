Desmond Tutu was the Archbishop of Cape Town following Philip Russell and being succeeded by Njongonkulu Ndungane.

Desmond Tutu and his family still live in South Africa.

Desmond Tutu is still the acting archbishop of Cape Town.

Desmond Tutu has been married most of his life.

Desmond Tutu was the Bishop of Johannesburg before he became the Archbishop of Cape Town.

Desmond Tutu is the current and presiding Archbishop of Cape Town.

Desmond Tutu had a ten year tenure in the Anglican Church of Southern Africa.

Desmond Tutu's tenure was longer than that of his predececssor Philip Russell.

Desmond Tutu is not a birth name, but a nickname, acquired during education in London.
