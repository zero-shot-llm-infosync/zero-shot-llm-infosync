The Moto G's removable storage can be expanded with a 16 GB microSDXC.

The Moto G was the best selling device in Motorola's 3rd Generation devices, which also included the Moto X Style and the Moto X Play.

The last OS available for the Moto G was Android 7.0 "Nougat."

The third generation Moto G allowed users to expand the memory via a microSD card slot.

Subsequent generations of the 3rd generation Moto G offered greater storage options.

The 3rd generation Moto G is the latest offering from the manufacturer.

The Moto G is a phone that has been discontinued.

The Moto G still is supported today.
